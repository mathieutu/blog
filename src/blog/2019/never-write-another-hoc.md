A video from <twl id="mjackson">Michael Jackson</twl>, creator of react-router library, 
where he explains the history of High Order Components, 
and why they should be avoid in the benefit of render props.

<url-preview url="https://youtube.com/watch?v=BcVAq3YFiuc" />

::: tip FYI
In VueJs, Render Props are also called [Scoped Slots](https://vuejs.org/v2/guide/components-slots.html#Scoped-Slots).
:::

### EDIT: 

From this time, React team have released Hooks, which allows a complete new way to handle component writing.
This video from <twl id="kentcdodds">Kent C. Dodds</twl> shows step by step how to refactor components 
from render prop to hooks:
